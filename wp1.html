Information about Virat Kohli


The modern-day legend, also known as Chase Master

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India.

Early Life:

Kohli grew up in Delhi and took a keen interest in cricket from a young age. He made his debut for the Delhi Under-15 team at the age of 14 and quickly rose through the ranks. He made his first-class debut for Delhi in 2006.

Career:

Kohli became the captain of the Indian national cricket team in all formats. He is one of the fastest players to score 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). He holds several records, including the fastest century in ODIs by an Indian and being the only player to score more than 70 international centuries.

Awards:

Arjuna Award (2013), Padma Shri (2017), ICC Player of the Year (2017), and many more.

Personal Life:

Kohli married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in December 2017. The couple has a daughter, Vamika.
